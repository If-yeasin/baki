# Bengali/English Copy Review Checklist

Updated: 2026-07-04

Status: prepared checklist only. Native Bengali copy review is not claimed until
a reviewer signs off with date, build, and notes.

## Scope

- Auth and OTP screens.
- Create/join khata.
- Group detail, group settings, invite copy/share/regeneration.
- Add/edit/delete expense, split methods, validation errors.
- Balances and simplified/raw-balance settlement views.
- bKash/Nagad/cash/other settlement handoff and no-payment-processing copy.
- Activity feed.
- Settings: sync repair, export, notifications, privacy, terms, support, delete account.
- Error toasts and empty states.

## Review Criteria

- Bengali is natural for Bangladesh, not overly formal or machine-translated.
- English and Bengali have the same meaning, especially around money and deletion.
- Baki never implies it processes, holds, authorizes, lends, or transfers money.
- OTP, invite-code, sync, and delete-account errors use distinct copy.
- Long Bengali strings fit compact buttons, rows, tabs, and status pills.
- Bengali numerals appear where expected for counts, dates, and money.
- Accessibility labels describe the action, not only the visible icon.
- Support/privacy copy tells users not to share OTPs, JWTs, full MFS numbers, or payment refs.

## Evidence To Record

- Reviewer name.
- Review date.
- App build or commit SHA.
- Locale tested.
- Device sizes or screenshots checked.
- Required copy changes.
- Sign-off decision.
