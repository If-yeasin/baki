# Baki Terms Draft

Last updated: 2026-07-02

Baki is a shared expense ledger. It is not a wallet, bank, payment processor, lender, or money transfer service.

## User Responsibilities

- Enter accurate expenses, shares, and settlement records.
- Confirm payment details before opening bKash, Nagad, cash, or other settlement handoffs.
- Use invite codes only with people who should access the khata.
- Settle balances directly with other members outside Baki.

## Ledger Records

Baki calculates balances from the records group members enter. If a mistake is found, members should edit or delete the expense, or record a correcting settlement.

## Offline Sync

Baki supports offline entry and later replay. Users should review failed sync items before dismissing them, because dismissing a failed ledger item removes it from that device's replay queue.

## Account Deletion

Account deletion is available in Settings after the user's own active balances are settled. Shared ledger history remains for other members.
